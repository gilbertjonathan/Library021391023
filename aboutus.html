Jonathan's Library is one of Australia's leading academic libraries, with a long-standing reputation for technological innovation and excellence in customer service. There are over 4.7 million items in our collection, including:

  • 3 million physical items
  • 1.6 million electronic items

Our services make a critical contribution to the achievement of Australia's education and research strategies.

About

Jonathan's Library acknowledges the traditional lands of all the Victorian Aboriginal clans, and their cultural practices and knowledge systems. We recognise that our collections hold traditional cultural knowledge belonging to Indigenous communities in Victoria and around the country.
